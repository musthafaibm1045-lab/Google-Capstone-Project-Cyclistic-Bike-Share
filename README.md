# # Cyclistic Bike-Share Analysis Case Study 🚲

**[span_1](start_span)Author:** Muhammad Musthafa[span_1](end_span)

## 📊 Project Overview
[span_2](start_span)This project analyzes the historical trip data of Cyclistic, a fictional bike-share company in Chicago[span_2](end_span). [span_3](start_span)The primary objective is to understand the behavioral differences between casual riders (Customers) and annual members (Subscribers)[span_3](end_span). [span_4](start_span)The insights gathered will help the marketing team design data-driven strategies to convert casual riders into profitable annual members[span_4](end_span).

## 🎯 Objectives
* [span_5](start_span)Understand the riding habits of Customers and Subscribers[span_5](end_span).
* [span_6](start_span)Understand the difference in rider numbers between the two groups[span_6](end_span).
* [span_7](start_span)Identify factors that influence casual riders to choose a subscription plan[span_7](end_span).

## 🛠️ Tools & Methodology
* **[span_8](start_span)Programming Language:** Python[span_8](end_span)
* **[span_9](start_span)IDE:** PyCharm Community[span_9](end_span)
* **[span_10](start_span)Libraries:** Pandas (Data manipulation), Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data visualization)[span_10](end_span)
* **[span_11](start_span)[span_12](start_span)Methodology:** Data from four quarters of 2019 were merged, cleaned (whitespace removal, datatype transformation), and aggregated by categories such as season, month, and weekday[span_11](end_span)[span_12](end_span).

## 💡 Key Findings
* **[span_13](start_span)User Distribution:** 77.1% of riders are annual subscribers, while 22.9% are casual customers[span_13](end_span).
* **[span_14](start_span)[span_15](start_span)Daily Trends:** Subscribers ride more frequently on weekdays, correlating with standard work hours[span_14](end_span)[span_15](end_span). [span_16](start_span)Conversely, casual customers ride predominantly on weekends[span_16](end_span).
* **[span_17](start_span)Seasonal Trends:** Summer (specifically July) sees the highest volume of rides, while Winter (specifically January) sees the lowest[span_17](end_span). 
* **[span_18](start_span)Ride Duration:** Casual customers tend to take longer rides, whereas subscribers generally take shorter trips (lasting less than 15 minutes)[span_18](end_span).

## 🚀 Recommendations
* **[span_19](start_span)Holiday/Weekend Focus:** Implement attractive subscription plans tailored for weekend and holiday riders to encourage casual customers to subscribe[span_19](end_span).
* **[span_20](start_span)Seasonal Pricing:** Consider reducing charges slightly during the slow winter months, while slightly increasing them during the busy summer season[span_20](end_span).
* **[span_21](start_span)Value Proposition:** Highlight the economic benefits of an annual subscription by making it notably more cost-effective compared to one-time use charges[span_21](end_span).
